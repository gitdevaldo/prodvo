The process

From idea to live product
in minutes

No configuration. No DevOps rabbit holes. Describe what you're building — Prodvo handles everything else.

01
Describe your product

Type what you want to build in plain language. "A task manager with team invites and Stripe billing." That's enough. Prodvo gets it.

02
Watch it build

The AI agent scaffolds the codebase, wires auth, creates your database schema, and builds the UI — live in your browser. You watch every step.

03
Iterate by talking

"Change the dashboard layout." "Add CSV export." "Make it mobile-friendly." The agent understands context — it evolves what's there, not restart.

04
Ship with one click

Hit deploy. Your app goes live on a custom domain with SSL, CDN, and zero configuration. It really is just one click.
