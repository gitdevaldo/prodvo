Common questions

Everything you
need to know.

Can I actually replace Vercel, Supabase, and Auth0 with just Prodvo?

Yes — completely. Prodvo includes a full Postgres database, zero-config authentication (email, OAuth, magic links), edge deployment with SSL and CDN, and transactional email. These are production-grade, not toys. Thousands of builders have cancelled their Vercel and Supabase subscriptions after switching.

What happens when I hit the limits on a free plan?

Your projects keep running — you just can't create new ones or use more AI prompts until the month resets. We never take down a live project because you hit a soft limit. When you're ready to grow, upgrading takes 30 seconds.

Is there a long-term contract or can I cancel anytime?

Monthly plans cancel immediately with no penalties. Annual plans are billed once a year upfront — if you cancel mid-year, you keep access through the end of your billing period. No fine print, no cancellation fees.

Can I export my project and host it somewhere else?

Always. Every project is standard code — Next.js, Prisma, standard Postgres. You can export the full codebase, database dump, and environment config at any time. We're not a lock-in platform. We'd rather earn your business every month than trap you.

Does the AI agent have access to my code and data?

The AI agent operates within your workspace only. Your code and data are isolated per workspace, encrypted at rest and in transit. We do not train models on your project data. Your intellectual property stays yours.

How does the annual billing discount work?

Annual billing gives you 20% off every plan — Pro drops from $29 to $23/mo, Team from $99 to $79/mo. You pay once upfront for the year. The savings on Pro cover themselves in about 2.5 months.
